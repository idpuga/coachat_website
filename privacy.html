Data Collection

We collect user data solely to improve your experience with Coachat. The data collected is exclusively used for personalization and optimization purposes. Your data will never be shared with third parties, and even our team will not have direct access to your information.

Data Encryption

All data collected from our users is encrypted to ensure your privacy and security. We take strong measures to protect your data, preventing unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

The data you enter into Coachat is processed by third-party APIs that help enhance your experience. Please be aware that these third parties have their own privacy policies, and we recommend you review them for further information. Coachat currently uses:

  • OpenAI API - OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Google Cloud API - Google Privacy Policy
