About Coachat

Coachat is an on-demand virtual coach, designed to help you navigate any type of problem or enhance any aspect of your life. Whether you're looking to achieve personal growth or find clarity, Coachat is here to guide you with an ontological coaching approach through a simple conversation interface.

Key Features

  • Self-development approach with personalized coaching.
  • Ontological approach focusing on the coachee's growth.
  • Send and receive both written and audio messages.
  • Trained categories of goals for targeted improvement.
  • Address selected emotions effectively with customized guidance.

Use Cases

  • Career Decisions: Get guidance to make informed career choices aligned with your values.
  • Relationship Improvement: Develop effective communication strategies to improve personal relationships.
  • Overcoming Fears: Address and overcome irrational fears with personalized coaching techniques.
  • Goal Setting: Set clear and actionable goals for personal and professional development.
  • Emotional Management: Understand and manage complex emotions effectively with the help of Coachat.
